## What's "Gloriously Awkward Life"? 

The original idea came from from [Jeff Foxworthy](https://www.pbs.org/wnet/americanmasters/jeff-foxworthy-interview/33231/), who defined the term "redneck" as "a glorious absence of sophistication." 

The fact is, I suck at video games, so one of my earlier handles was `GloriousAbsenceofSkill`. It stuck for a while, viewers and friends got a huge kick out of it. I still have it on a couple of Christian stream team tee shirts.

But gaming on its own became boring and I wanted to do more writing and blogging, given where I'm located (hello, Disney World is less than a half-hour away). So I chose `GloriouslyAwkwardLife`. I've also gone much more into things like creating and 3D printing rather than games. I no longer have a gaming PC; I switched to Apple _everything_ last year. 

As I started reestablishing my roots over the last year or so here in east-central Florida, which included finding a [new church home](https://44life.com) and becoming a partner with them, I found that the Christian life itself could best be described as "awkward" compared to the life that "the world" seeks to live. 

Paul put it best, I suppose:

> For the message of the cross is foolishness [absurd and illogical] to those who are perishing and spiritually dead [because they reject it], but to us who are being saved [by God’s grace] it is [the manifestation of] the power of God.  
> &mdash;1 Corinthians 1:8, Amplified Bible

To "the world," I'm awkward; I'm also awkward simply because I'm type 1 autistic as well.  But because of the cross I'm _gloriously_ awkward.

So that's what I'm about&mdash;living a _gloriously awkward life._ And that's how this name and handle came about.
