I’m a type I autistic 50-something living on my own near Walt Disney World; and yes—I’m a Passholder. I also spend a lot of time working with all the data that is generated by my devices (smartphone, smartwatch, health apps, lifelogging apps, *&c.*) to analyze and publish my own personal informatics, and share other data that I think is of interest.

Playing with data is “in my blood,” I suppose. I work from home full-time as a reporting analyst and Power BI developer. I relocated to Florida in 2000 and have lived in Lakeland, Plant City, and Tampa before settling in my current location.

My *formal* training is a 1996 B.A. *cum laude* in religion with emphasis in pastoral ministry from Vanguard University of Southern California (VUSC), but life and my own failings had other ideas. I’ve also done some French language and culture study by correspondence with the Alliance Française in Paris, and studied French, business, and computer science at Coastline College in California before transferring to VUSC.
