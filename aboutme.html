I'm a type I autistic 50-something living on my own near Walt Disney World; and yes, I'm a passholder, at Walt Disney World and at all of the SeaWorld and Busch Gardens theme parks, with my "home park" being SeaWorld Orlando.

I also spend a lot of time working with all the data that is generated by my devices (smartphone, smartwatch, health apps, lifelogging apps, &c.) to analyze and publish my own personal informatics, and share other data that I think is of interest. I use a combination of Python and Wolfram Language to compile daily logs and data visualizations that you see on this site.

Playing with data is “in my blood,” I suppose. I work from home (well, pretty much anywhere) full-time as a reporting analyst and Microsoft Power BI and SQL Server Reporting Services developer. I relocated to Florida in 2000 and have lived in Lakeland, Plant City, and Tampa before settling in my current location here near the Disney World tourist trap.

My formal training earned me a 1996 B.A. cum laude (with honors) in religion with emphasis in pastoral ministry from Vanguard University of Southern California (VUSC), but life and my own failings had other ideas. I’ve also done some French language and culture study by correspondence with the Alliance Française in Paris, and studied French, business, and computer science at Coastline College in California before transferring to VUSC.
