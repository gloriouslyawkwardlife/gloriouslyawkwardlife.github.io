This was recorded on Christmas Eve 2023 after the evening fireworks were done and guests were making their way out of the park for home or their hotels to have Christmas with their families.

Peter Pan's Flight is pretty much like the original at Disneyland. The wait times are notoriously long (75 minutes or more) because it is the lowest capacity of all the rides in the Walt Disney World parks. In this case the wait was around 45 minutes because of the season.
