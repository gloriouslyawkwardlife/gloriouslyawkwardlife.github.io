title: A Prayer Before Preaching
author: Steven Buehler
date: 2024-06-23
categories: ["Poetry & Writings"]
---

My Lord,  
I would be the first to confess that I have no idea what I'm doing here.  
I only know that you have ordained this moment, this place, and somehow this vessel to say something to your people.  
These racing thoughts, these fears, these anxieties:  
Still them,  
So that I may focus and speak no more and no less  
than what you want to say.  
Don't let me be in your way.
