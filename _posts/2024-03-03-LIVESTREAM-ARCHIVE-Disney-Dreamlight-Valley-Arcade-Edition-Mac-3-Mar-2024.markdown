For some reason my March 2nd stream via iPhone did not get saved by Twitch; in that session I opened up access to the Lagoon on Eternity Isle.

These are my archived Twitch broadcasts of the **Apple Arcade Edition** of "Disney Dreamlight Valley", which has some differences from the Gameloft paid editions for Mac, PC, and Consoles:  

- Moonstones and microtransactions are unavailable;
- The hidden Potato Quests are unavailable;
- The Apple Arcade Edition cannot be synced with the Gameloft paid version. 

The plus side: This can be played on iPhone and iPad as well as Mac, and otherwise paid expansions are included. 🤓
