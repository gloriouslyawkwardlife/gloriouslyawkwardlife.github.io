Below are web and other resources that I'm reading on the regular.  It's not complete and it's mostly in alphabetical rather than preferential order. 

# Feeds

- [Armstrong Economics](https://www.armstrongeconomics.com/)&mdash;creator of the Socrates computation-based cyclical model that has, to date, _never_ been wrong.
- [The Babylon Bee](https://babylonbee.com/)&mdash;political and cultural satire; _The Onion_ for religious folk.
- [Desiring God](https://www.desiringgod.org)&mdash;The online home of John Piper.
- [Lifeway Research](https://research.lifeway.com/)&mdash;research and insight into the trajectory of today's Christian churches.
- [Modernity](https://modernity.news/)&mdash;conservative news and opinion.
- [Mitch Teemly](https://mitchteemley.com/)&mdash;in a past life, was one part of the Christian comedy group Isaac Air Freight. Is now a writer and producer.
- [Walt Disney World Resort Parks Blog](https://disneyparksblog.com/wdw/)&mdash;well duh, I have a pass...
- [Wolfram Blog](https://blog.wolfram.com/)&mdash;perhaps my favorite software product in the world.
- [Stephen Wolfram: Writings](https://writings.stephenwolfram.com/)&mdash;if you think _I'm_ OCD...
- [ZeroHedge News](https://cms.zerohedge.com/fullrss2.xml)&mdash;curated news and opinion on the financial markets.
